Our Story

Two decades of market expertise.

Founded at London's New Spitalfields Market by seasoned professionals with over 20 years of experience, RavegLtd has grown into one of the market's largest importers and wholesalers of Indian and Afro-Caribbean fresh fruit and vegetables.

Our mission has always been simple — supply local retailers with top-quality produce from around the world. That dedication to consistency, service and excellence has earned us a strong reputation among supermarkets and independent shops alike.

What We Offer

A dependable, year-round supply.

We source a wide variety of fresh fruit and vegetables from trusted farms worldwide, giving us a dependable, year-round supply of both everyday essentials and specialised produce.

We're proud to be recognised as market leaders in Afro-Caribbean and Indian fruit and veg — from plantains and cassava to exotic mangoes and okra — the go-to wholesaler for businesses serving diverse communities.
